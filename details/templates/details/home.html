#PaintYourBlues

Do you believe in the power of art?

To heal, shift moods, shape opinions, silence stigma and spur movements?
Join #PaintYourBlues, a poster campaign to create awareness on key aspects of mental health -
self care, peer to peer support and seeking professional health.
Six entries (3 curator + 3 community picks) will be awarded Rs.10,000 each.


Themes

#1 Selfcare

refers to the thoughts, actions and behaviour of an individual to take control and care of their mental health leading to better well-being outcomes.

In practice :

Getting 6-8 hrs of sleep, cultivating a hobby, being spiritual, exercising, meditating, being self-compassionate, setting aside me-time, and learning how to say no.
